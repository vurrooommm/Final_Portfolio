About Me

Hi, I'm Renier R. Tamayo, an Illustrator and Animator with a passion for creating vibrant and engaging art. With several years of experience in digital illustration and animation, I specialize in bringing stories to life through dynamic visuals and creative storytelling. I am currently stationed in Sampaloc, Manila originally from Oton, Iloilo.

My journey in digital art began just a few years ago, and I've since developed expertise in various digital tools and techniques. I take pride in creating artwork that not only captures attention but also conveys meaningful messages and emotions.
